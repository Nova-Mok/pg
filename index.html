How PropertyGuard Works 🏡🔒

Managing your property has never been easier! PropertyGuard simplifies the process of protecting, monitoring, and maintaining your property. From registration to real-time updates and instant alerts, we ensure your property is always secure and under expert supervision. Whether you're nearby or miles away, we've got you covered.
Get started

Claim Your Space

Sign up and register your property with essential details like location, size, and ownership documents. This step secures your property in our system, ready for round-the-clock monitoring.

Receive detailed reports after every visit, complete with visuals and expert observations. These reports keep you informed about your property’s condition and any potential concerns.
